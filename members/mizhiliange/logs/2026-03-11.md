## Errors and How I Resolved Them

### Error 1

**Description**:
Local `container run` failed repeatedly with `ColumnNotFoundError` for many trace fields.

**Steps taken**:
I tried to manually mock missing Parquet columns (`Timestamp`, `TraceId`, `SpanKind`, etc.) based on error messages.

**Resolution**: 
Eventually realized that rcabench-platform v2 container mode expects a fully valid official dataset schema and is not suitable for hand-written mock data.
